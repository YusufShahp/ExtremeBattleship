Click the button to enter the battle. Good luck!

How to Play

1. Place your ships. When you start the game keep rearanging ships until they are in a satisfactory position

2. Guess in the top half to find and sink all of the opponent's (computer or person) ships

A red dot means you hit a ship, a white dot means you missed, once you hit all points on a ship it will turn dark red

The opponent will also guess to hit your ships, look at the bottom to see where the opponent is guessing and which ships are sunk

(the opponents's most recent guess will apear blinking)

4. If you sink all the opponent's ships before it sinks all of yours you win, if you don't the opponent wins
